The Perfect Tool for Livebloggers

Inspired by Zach Tschida, Cheetah Blog is an easy to use flowing program that posts arguments to your Twitter account, with speech and time information added.

Click on the green button to go to Twitter to enter your credentials. You'll then be taken to the flow application.
